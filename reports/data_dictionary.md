# Data Dictionary

## Fund Master

amfi_code - Unique scheme identifier

scheme_name - Name of mutual fund scheme

fund_house - Asset Management Company

category - Fund category

sub_category - Detailed category

risk_category - Risk classification

## NAV History

amfi_code - Scheme code

date - NAV date

nav - Net Asset Value

## AUM

aum_crore - Assets under management in crores

## SIP

sip_inflow_crore - SIP inflow amount

active_sip_accounts - Active SIP accounts
